Welcome to Tripoli!

Introduction

Tripoli is the second largest and one of the oldest cities in Lebanon; it's at least 34 centuries old in fact! Consequently, it has some of the oldest artifacts and historical sites in the world. It's commonly known for housing the Mansouri Great Mosque and the Citadel of Raymond de Saint-Gilles (Qal'at Sinjil), alongside a great concentration of Mamluk Architecture. It has very friendly people that will welcome you with open arms.
